Who We Are

Aspect Foundation was founded in 2016 as a Non-Government & Non-Profit Organization Working to Empower Education or Poor In Both Rural & Urban Areas.We are working for empowering women, helping poor and needy people, provide education to them,Prevent Child labour. A team of 100 members is working across all over Madhya Pradesh. All the members are supportive and dedicated about helping others.

Why Join Us

Working for an NGO can be a challenging activity & it is better than sitting at home dreaming about an ideal society you want to live in.The only way to achieve an ideal society is,to make one,by working positively towards societal development.
